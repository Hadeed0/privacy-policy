Introduction

Subtitle Learner ("we," "us," or "our") operates Subtitle Learner, which uses WebView to load YouTube and Google Translate. Additionally, our app utilizes speech recognition to convert user speech into text for translation purposes in Google Translate WebView. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1.1 Personal Information

Speech Data: Our app collects audio data when you use the speech recognition feature. This data is converted into text and used for translation purposes within the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Crash Reports: We collect crash logs to help us improve the app and fix any issues.

1.3 Future Data Collection

We do not currently collect any additional personal data. However, we may decide to collect additional types of data in the future. If we do, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly to reflect those changes.

2. How We Use Your Information
