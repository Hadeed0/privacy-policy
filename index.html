Introduction

Subtitle Learner ("we," "us," or "our") operates Subtitle Learner, which uses WebView to load YouTube and Google Translate. Additionally, our app utilizes speech recognition to convert user speech into text for translation purposes in Google Translate WebView. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Speech Data

Speech Data: Our app temporarily collects audio data when you use the speech recognition feature. This data is converted into text and used for translation purposes within the app. The audio data is not stored and is discarded after the translation is processed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services, including speech recognition and translation features.
  • Generate revenue through ads displayed in the app.

3. How We Share Your Information

The data collected by AdMob is subject to their own privacy policies. For more information, please refer to the AdMob Privacy Policy.
