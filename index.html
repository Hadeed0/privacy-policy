Introduction

Subtitle Learner ("we," "us," or "our") operates Subtitle Learner, which uses WebView to load YouTube and Google Translate. Additionally, our app utilizes speech recognition to convert user speech into text for translation purposes in Google Translate WebView. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Speech Data: Our app collects audio data when you use the speech recognition feature. This data is converted into text and used for translation purposes within the app.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our app, such as the features you use and the duration of your activities.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device Information: We collect information about your device, including the device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Log Data: Our servers automatically collect certain information when you use our app, including your IP address, browser type, and access times.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services, including speech recognition and translation features.
  • Personalize your experience by customizing the content you see.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve the app's functionality and user experience.
  • Ensure the security of our services and prevent fraud.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Services

Google Translate: We use Google Translate for translation services. The text generated from speech recognition is sent to Google Translate to provide translations.

YouTube: Our app loads YouTube videos through WebView. YouTube may collect data as described in their privacy policy.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
